Read About us

Agriculture, according to Mahatma Gandhi, is the cornerstone of the Indian economy. This is still true today because agriculture continues to be the main source of income in rural areas and is essential to maintaining the economy as a whole. About 52% of the Indian population works in it, and it provides 16% to the country's overall GDP. The agriculture sector's explosive growth is essential for both achieving self-sufficiency and earning lucrative foreign exchange. Indian farmers can assure the country's food and nutritional security by providing timely and sufficient inputs including fertilizers, seeds, pesticides, inexpensive agricultural financing, and crop insurance. The goal is to employ information and communication technology to give pertinent information and services to the farming community and the private sector as a supplement to the department's current delivery routes. The Farmers' Portal is a start toward developing an all-in-one resource for all agricultural, animal husbandry, and fisheries-related information and requirements of Indian farmers. Farmers are no longer required to search through several websites made for certain reasons. Farmers will have access to thorough information on specific topics pertaining to their village, block, district, or state through the Farmers' Portal. The farmer will receive this information in his or her native tongue via text, SMS, email, audio, and video forms. Farmers can readily obtain information at many levels by just clicking on the India map on the homepage. Farmers will also have the chance to ask specific questions and offer insightful feedback via a dedicated Feedback module.
